The following conditions shall be applicable:

  • For undergraduate Scholarships, the required CGPA must be earned with a minimum load of 12 Cr.Hrs.
  • For Graduate Scholarship, the required CGPA should be attained with a minimum load of 6 Cr.Hrs.
  • Scholarships are awarded in the form of tuition fee waiver; they are not applicable on admission fee or any other charges of the University unless specifically stated.
  • No Scholarship is applicable on Thesis/Project and on summer registration.
  • The Scholarship will be awarded only within normal degree duration and is not applicable on extra semester(s) for whatsoever reasons.
  • A Specially-abled student if not meeting the CGPA eligibility criterion, shall be awarded 25% reduced scholarship if the CGPA is below the required CGPA but higher than the CGPA required for the award of degree.
  • Merit Scholarship for new admissions are awarded based on available result, if the applicant is result awaiting.
  • Applicants admitted in 5th semester after 14-year /ADP qualification shall be considered for scholarships by comparing their CGPA with the continuation CGPA of the corresponding category.
  • The University, vide its discretionary power, has the authority to change the definition or percentage of any Scholarship, with the approval of the Academic Council, and it shall be binding on all awardees.
  • No Scholarship is applicable on a repeat course or a course registered for improvement.
  • Student convicted by the Discipline Committee will be ineligible for any Scholarship/Award.
